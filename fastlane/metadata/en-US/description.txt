Orbit is your personal relationship intelligence companion. It helps you maintain and strengthen your connections with friends, family, colleagues, and everyone in between.

Your relationships are your most valuable asset, but life gets busy. Orbit makes sure no one falls through the cracks.

KEY FEATURES:

Relationship Health Scores
Each contact gets a dynamic health score based on interaction frequency, depth, and reciprocity. Watch your relationships flourish or catch them before they fade.

Smart Nudges
Get personalized reminders about who to reach out to, with AI-powered conversation starters tailored to each relationship.

AI-Powered Insights
Understand your social patterns with intelligent summaries and actionable suggestions powered by Claude AI.

Gamified Motivation
Level up your social skills with XP, quests, achievements, and a Solo Leveling-inspired ranking system. Complete daily quests, join parties, and climb the hunter ranks.

Visual Orbit View
See all your relationships mapped in an interactive orbital visualization. Inner circle, close friends, acquaintances - everyone has their place.

Social Challenges
Challenge yourself with gates, boss raids, and streak challenges to keep your connections active.

Life Events Tracking
Log birthdays, moves, job changes, and other milestones to always have context for your conversations.

Interaction Logging
Track calls, meetups, messages, and more. Orbit learns your patterns and adapts its recommendations.

Privacy First
Your relationship data stays private and secure. No social media scraping, no contact sharing.
