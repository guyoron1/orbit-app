Frequently Asked Questions

How does relationship health work?
Each relationship has a health score (0-100%) that naturally decays over time based on your desired contact frequency. When you log an interaction, health goes back up. The algorithm learns from your patterns and adapts the decay curve to each relationship.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your relationship data is encrypted and never shared with third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time. We use AI features locally where possible and only use OpenAI for optional conversation starters.
What are quests and parties?
Quests are gamified challenges that encourage you to nurture specific relationships ("Call a friend you haven't spoken to in 2 weeks"). Parties let you create group activities and earn bonus XP when you hang out with multiple people.
Can I import my existing contacts?
Yes! Upload a CSV file with your contacts and we'll import them automatically. You can also add contacts manually or connect integrations like Strava for activity tracking.
Does Orbit work offline?
Orbit is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your home screen and basic features work offline. Interactions logged offline will sync when you're back online.

  • Interaction Logs: Records of interactions you log, including type (call, text, in-person, etc.), duration, notes, and timestamps.
  • Life Events: Milestones and events you record for your contacts (birthdays, job changes, etc.).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns within the app to improve the Service.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution for responsive design optimization.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Danger Zone. This action is immediate and irreversible.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data — including contacts, interactions, notes, life events, gamification progress, and account information — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely.
